# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md
### Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Pledge
We commit to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age  
- Level of experience  
- Gender identity  
- Race  
- Religion  
- Technical skill  
- Nationality  

We want all contributors to feel safe and respected.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

- Be respectful  
- Use inclusive language  
- Accept constructive criticism  
- Focus on collaboration  
- Be patient with newcomers  

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks  
- Hate speech  
- Posting private information  
- Disruptive, toxic, or antagonistic behavior  
- Sexualized language or imagery  

---

## 4. Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness a violation, report it by opening an issue or contacting the project owner privately.

Maintainers must act quickly, fairly, and respectfully.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Violators may face:

- Warning  
- Temporary ban  
- Permanent ban  

depending on severity.

---

Thank you for helping us build a respectful and open community.
